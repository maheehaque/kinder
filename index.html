About Us

Located in the heart of Harris Park, Mahee Smith Kindergarten provides a warm, safe, and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive through guided play and exploration.

As a private initiative driven by values of social justice and community care, we welcome all children and families with open arms. Our experienced educators are dedicated to creating a nurturing space that celebrates each child's unique potential.

Our facility features:

  • Spacious indoor and outdoor play areas
  • Age-appropriate learning resources
  • Nutritious meal options
  • Multicultural learning environment

Our Programs

We offer a variety of early learning programs focused on creativity, language development, social interaction, and physical activity. Our approach is child-centered and designed to prepare children for a successful school journey.

Creative Explorers

For ages 3-4, focusing on art, music, and imaginative play to develop creativity and fine motor skills.

Language Learners

For ages 4-5, emphasizing literacy, communication, and early reading skills through engaging activities.

Active Adventurers

For all ages, promoting physical development through outdoor play, sports, and movement activities.
